The problem

Traditional hiring is
structurally broken

Top candidates are off the market in 10 days. Your hiring process takes 45. You're not losing people to better companies — you're losing them to your own workflow.

01

Resume screening takes forever

Recruiters spend 4–6 hours per role reading resumes. By application 200, pattern-matching replaces judgment. Bias enters through the back door.

02

Portfolios are reviewed by non-designers

A keyword scanner can't tell the difference between beautiful screens with no UX thinking and rough wireframes backed by deep user research. Generic tools miss what matters.

03

AI-generated applications are invisible

Candidates increasingly use AI to generate entire resumes and case study narratives. Fluent text masks fabricated experience. Existing tools don't even check.
